What is Ledgera?

Ledgera is an AI-powered compliance management platform built for Indian Chartered Accountants. It tracks every GST, TDS, ITR, and ROC deadline across your entire client portfolio, scans documents with on-device OCR, and uses a local Gemma 3 AI model to predict government notices before they arrive.

The desktop app runs everything locally on your machine — no data leaves your device unless you choose to sync. The web companion at app.ledgera.co.in lets you check status from any browser, but the full AI features live in the desktop app.
